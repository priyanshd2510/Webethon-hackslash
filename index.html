Vision Of DSC

Google DSC is a Developer Student Club which is a technical community that combines all the university students, and all the other students who learn, share ideas and come up with viable projects that are likely to solve day to day universe problems. Additionally, it aims for university students to help them build their mobile, web, machine learning, cloud skills.
